> **Ahamie** — pronounced **"ah-HAH-mee"**, rhymes with **salami**.

## Etymology

The Tamil root *aham* (அகம், meaning *self / inner core*) is hidden inside the spelling. A user's "company brain" should feel like an inner core — not a vendor's tool, but their own.

We deliberately switched off "Akamie" to pre-empt audible collision with Akamai (different prefix, different consonant cluster). USPTO classes 009 (software) and 042 (SaaS) are clean.

## Stylization

- Lowercase **ahamie** in code and CLI
- Capitalized **Ahamie** in prose, marketing, and trademark filings
- The wordmark uses no special characters or diacritics — ASCII-stable across keyboards
- The logomark renders the *aham* syllable as a softly highlighted inner core

## Tagline

> *the company brain you own.*
